Codebook of variables:
======================

subject -- number (from 1 to 30) identifying an individual test subject.

activity -- a descriptor of the given activity that the person was 
            supposed to carry out.  

remaining fields -- means (mean) and standard deviations (std) of 
            various orientations, and motion parameters (e.g. 
            Acc = acceleration, Jerk = sudden motion)

See description files in the dataset downloadable from:
http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones
and,
https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip
